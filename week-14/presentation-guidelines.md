## Presentation Overview

This is the first of two presentation sessions where students demonstrate their understanding of course concepts through applied projects.

---

## Presentation Requirements

### Format

- **Duration**: 15-20 minutes per student/group
- **Q&A**: 5 minutes after each presentation
- **Slides**: PowerPoint, Google Slides, or PDF
- **Code Demo**: Encouraged (Marimo notebooks preferred)

### Content Structure

1. **Problem Statement** (2-3 min)
   - What problem are you solving?
   - Why is it important?
   - What data are you using?

2. **Methodology** (5-7 min)
   - What models/algorithms did you use?
   - Reference relevant textbook chapters
   - Explain your approach

3. **Implementation** (3-5 min)
   - Key code snippets or demo
   - Tools and libraries used
   - Challenges encountered

4. **Results** (3-5 min)
   - Metrics and evaluation
   - Visualizations
   - Comparison of approaches

5. **Conclusions** (2-3 min)
   - Key findings
   - Limitations
   - Future work
